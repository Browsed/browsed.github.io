Browsed

Discover most interesting web links browsed from your people in last 24 hours.

A new way of discovering web

A chrome extension that analyses your browsing history to curate 10 most interesting web links discovered in past 24 hours and automatically sharing them with your people.

01

Automatic curation

Self-learning algorithms update your personalised feed on regular basis so you are always in the know of your people.

02

Direct connection

Works fully contained within your browser and shares interesting links with your people directly, without any intermediaries.

03

Private data

Uses direct Peer-to-Peer data distribution to ensure utmost security and anonymisation of exact person, only revealing aggregated content.
